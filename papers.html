Selected works in QI

  • State of factoring in 2026: CPU/GPU/QPU analysis
    This project was about estimating cross-platform time and resource consumption in factoring on high-performance CPUs, GPU accelerators, and QPUs.
  • Quantum many-body physics: Classical and Quantum-Circuit Benchmarks for Dynamical Correlations in a 2D Heisenberg Magnet
    In this project, we propose an observable-level benchmark programme for dynamical spin correlations in small two-dimensional quantum magnets. The benchmark compares exact diagonalization and Krylov time evolution, MPS/TDVP under two-dimensional snake mappings, and Trotterized quantum-circuit simulation on common observables such as low-energy spectra and real-time spin correlations. We discuss how TDVP convergence, entanglement growth, and circuit depth define complementary reliability windows.
  • How to generate, measure entanglement in the lab
    Here, I studied how to generate entanglement and measure it. I gave examples of Quantum Optics and with Superconducting Qubits.
  • Entanglement as a resource for quantum computation and quantum networking
    Here, I studied why entanglement is so essential in quantum computation: implementation of quantum gates, 2 qubit gates, and controlled gates. Then, I also gaev clear answers why quantum networking is based on entanglement. I havev shown here the computations mathematically and by code.
